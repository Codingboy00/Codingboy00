One of the most popular whiskies in India, Royal Stag redefined the country’s spirits space on its launch in 1995. It pioneered blending Indian grain spirits and imported Scotch malts – the first whisky in India not to use any artificial flavourings and to offer an international quality blend. Part of Pernod Ricard since 2001, Royal Stag has become an iconic brand with a large and fiercely loyal following.
